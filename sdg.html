SDG 15: Life on Land

Forests are the backbone of life on Earth. SDG 15 focuses on protecting, restoring, and promoting sustainable use of forests while conserving biodiversity. Our mission aligns with this global goal by inspiring action at local and global levels.

Forest Conservation Goals

  • Raising Awareness: Educating people on the importance of forests and the dangers of deforestation.
  • Reducing Deforestation: Encouraging eco-friendly alternatives, regulating logging, and reducing wood waste.
  • Afforestation & Reforestation: Organizing plantation drives and restoring damaged forest land.
  • Community Participation: Involving local communities, students, NGOs, and volunteers.
  • Protecting Habitats: Conserving wildlife areas and safeguarding endangered species.
